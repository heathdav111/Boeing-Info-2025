Content

Home page

The home page will feature a full-width hero image of a Boeing aircraft. Below that will be three feature cards introducing the Boeing 737, Boeing 787. A large aircraft image and introductory text will explain the purpose of the site. The layout ends with a simple footer containing navigation links and social icons.
