Boeing 737

The Boeing 737 is the world’s most successful commercial jet aircraft. Designed for short- to medium-haul routes, it is used by airlines across the globe for its efficiency, reliability, and versatility.

Quick Facts

  • First flight: 1967
  • Typical seating: 126–220 passengers
  • Range: ~3,500 nautical miles
  • Engines: CFM International
